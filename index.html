Tyrone Jenkins

About Me

Tyrone Jenkins was born in Maryland while his father, then a Captain in the Air Force, was assigned to the Pentagon. As a typical Air Force brat, he lived in several states while growing up. Washington D.C., Hawaii, California, and lastly, Washington St. After high school, he attended Tuskegee University in Alabama, before transfering to Morehouse College in Atlanta Georgia. During his time in college, he enlisted in the United States Marine Corps - attending bootcamp at Paris Island S.C. Prior to finishing college, he was accepted to and joined the Marine Corps OCS program to be commissioned as a 2nd Lt upon graduation.
